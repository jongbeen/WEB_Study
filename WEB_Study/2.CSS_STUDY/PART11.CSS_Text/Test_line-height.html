Ryan Giggs has expressed his belief that Manchester United need three new starters in order to challenge for the Premier League title and Champions League next season. United secured a third-place finish in the top flight after a final-day victory over Leicester at the King Power Stadium. Ole Gunnar Solskjaer's side were way off the top-four pace at the turn of the year, but the January arrival of Bruno Fernandes sparked an impressive turnaround in fortunes.
